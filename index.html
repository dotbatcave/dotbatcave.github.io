_A Journey Exploring My Roots_



My name is Dot and i'm Créole from La Réunion




..i'm going to share with you what i love the most about my island..


i love how diverse and generous the Nature is

  • lush mountains
  • wild beaches
  • natural hot springs
  • and so many more breathtaking landscapes you cannot even imagine..

you can find so many amazing animals

  • the emblematic paille-en-queue for example
  • turtles, fishes, sea snails, urchins..
  • whales! sharks!
  • sooo many colourful corals




i love my language, Creole. And all the music that was created because of this mix of cultures (mostly from India, Madagascar and Africa).

we dance and play Sega, Maloya. this is our heritage from our Ancestors who were slaves, deported from their Mother Land




but what i looove the most about my island is....




...THE FOOD !!
